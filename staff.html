Michigan State Roleplay (MSR) Staff Guidebook

Welcome to the Michigan State Roleplay (MSR) staff team! As a staff member, you play a crucial role in ensuring a safe, fair, and enjoyable experience for all players. This guide outlines your responsibilities, expectations, and the standards we uphold within our community.

1. Trial Moderator
Role: Assist in monitoring the server, address minor issues, and learn from higher-ranking staff.
Responsibilities:
• Monitor chat for inappropriate content.
• Assist players with basic questions.
• Report significant issues to Moderators or higher.

2. Moderator
Role: Enforce server rules, handle player reports, and support Trial Moderators.
Responsibilities:
• Address rule violations promptly.
• Assist in training Trial Moderators.
• Participate in staff meetings and discussions.

3. Senior Moderator
Role: Oversee moderation activities, mentor junior staff, and contribute to policy development.
Responsibilities:
• Lead investigations into serious incidents.
• Provide feedback to improve staff performance.
• Assist in organizing events and community activities.

4. Administrator
Role: Manage server operations, implement policies, and ensure the community's well-being.
Responsibilities:
• Oversee all staff activities.
• Resolve escalated disputes and appeals.
• Coordinate with developers for server updates.

Professionalism: Maintain a respectful and impartial demeanor at all times. Avoid favoritism and treat all players equally.
Confidentiality: Keep internal matters private. Do not disclose sensitive information outside the staff team.
Activity: Be active and engaged. Inactivity without notice may result in removal from the staff team.
Communication: Use designated staff channels for coordination. Address conflicts internally before involving the broader community.

1. Handling Rule Violations
Minor Offenses (e.g., accidental RDM):
• Issue a verbal warning.
• Educate the player on proper conduct.
Major Offenses (e.g., intentional exploitation):
• Apply appropriate sanctions (e.g., kick, temporary ban).
• Document the incident for records.

2. Commands Usage
:kick [player] [reason]: Remove a player for a specified reason.
:ban [player] [duration] [reason]: Ban a player for a set duration.
:m [message]: Broadcast a message to the server.
Note: Use commands responsibly. Misuse can lead to disciplinary action.

Rule Adherence: Staff must follow all server rules. Violations by staff are taken seriously and may result in stricter penalties.
Bias Prevention: Avoid favoritism. Enforce rules consistently, regardless of personal relationships.
Language and Communication: Use clear and professional language. Avoid slang or informal speech in official capacities.

Ongoing Training: Participate in regular training sessions to stay updated on best practices and server policies.
Mentorship: Senior staff should mentor junior members, fostering a supportive learning environment.

Incident Reports: Document all significant incidents using the provided templates in the Discord. Accurate records help in decision-making and transparency.
Feedback: Provide constructive feedback to peers. Continuous improvement is a collective effort.
