Michigan State Roleplay (MSR) Staff Guidebook

Welcome to the Michigan State Roleplay (MSR) staff team! As a staff member, you play a crucial role in ensuring a safe, fair, and enjoyable experience for all players. This guide outlines your responsibilities, expectations, and the standards we uphold within our community.

1. **Trial Moderator**
Role: Assist in monitoring the server, address minor issues, and learn from higher-ranking staff.
Responsibilities:

  • Monitor chat for inappropriate content.
  • Assist players with basic questions.
  • Report significant issues to Moderators or higher.

2. **Moderator**
Role: Monitor chat, manage player behavior, and enforce rules.
Responsibilities:

  • Handle reports and issues escalated by Trial Moderators.
  • Assist players with advanced questions.
  • Take action against players who violate server rules.

3. **Senior Moderator**
Role: Lead the moderation team and ensure the smooth operation of the server.
Responsibilities:

  • Oversee the actions of Moderators and Trial Moderators.
  • Handle more severe player infractions and disputes.
  • Assist in training new staff members.

4. **Admin**
Role: Administer high-level decisions and ensure server integrity.
Responsibilities:

  • Make major decisions regarding server rules and policies.
  • Manage staff performance and issue promotions/demotions.
  • Enforce the rules at the highest level.

Professionalism: Maintain a respectful and impartial demeanor at all times. Avoid favoritism and treat all players equally.
Confidentiality: Staff members are expected to respect the privacy of players and not disclose any personal information shared within the staff team.
Teamwork: Work together to resolve issues, and always ask for assistance when needed. We are a team, and our goal is to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for all.

1. **Player Misconduct**
If a player is engaging in misconduct (e.g., exploiting, trolling), report it immediately to the Moderators.
Make sure to gather sufficient evidence to support the claim (screenshots, video recordings, etc.).
2. **Staff Misconduct**
If you encounter staff misconduct, report it to an Admin or higher. Staff members must hold themselves to a higher standard of behavior and conduct.
3. **Appeals Process**
If a player believes they have been wrongly punished, they can appeal their case. As staff, it is our responsibility to evaluate these appeals fairly and make unbiased decisions based on evidence.

1. **Warnings:**
Minor infractions (e.g., minor spamming, minor trolling) will result in a warning. Multiple warnings can lead to a temporary ban.
2. **Temporary Bans:**
Players who break rules severely or repeatedly will be temporarily banned (usually for 24-72 hours).
3. **Permanent Bans:**
Players who engage in severe misconduct (e.g., cheating, exploiting, or harassment) may face permanent bans.
4. **Demotions:**
Staff members who fail to uphold MSR’s standards or fail in their duties may be demoted or removed from their position.

Staff meetings are held weekly to discuss server updates, issues, and training. All staff members are expected to attend and participate. If you cannot attend, please inform an Admin or higher in advance.
